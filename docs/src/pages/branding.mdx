app-icon squircle; the wordmark is lowercase **dogfood** in SF Pro.

## Wordmark

Lowercase **dogfood**, SF Pro (`-apple-system`), weight 700, tracking −0.03em, ink `#1C1C1C`.

## Lockup

Mark + wordmark sit together with the mark height ≈ 1.4× the wordmark's lowercase height.
Keep clear space ≥ the mark's corner radius on every side.

## Color

| Token | Value |
|---|---|
| Ink (the mark) | `#1C1C1C` |
| Surface | `#FFFFFF` |
| Blue | `#007AFF` |
| Green | `#34C759` |

## Usage

- Use the **primary** mark on light surfaces and the **inverse** on dark — both are
  self-contained (they carry their own squircle).
- Keep the squircle's medium corners — they're tuned to pair with the wordmark.
- Don't recolor, stretch, rotate, or add effects to Cowboy.
- Don't place the dark mark on a dark background — use the inverse.
